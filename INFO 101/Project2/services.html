1. New PC Assembly

You can give a specification to our team of technicians, and they can build the computer from scratch for you. This means that you dont have to pay for things that you don't need, such as an operating system if you want to run Linux instead. The technicians can advise you on the best choice of hardware for your individual needs.

2. Spyware/mallware and Virus Removal

The internet is full of spyware/malware and viruses. These pose a very real security risk. They could keep a track of keystrokes and therefore have access to your bank account or other secure sites, posing as you. They also slow down the computer, by sending out information. These programs can dig themselves very deep into your computer and be very hard to completely get rid of once found. Our team will 'steam clean' your computer to return it good-as-new. We will also install a firewall, anti-virus and anti-spyware software. These can be freely tools or commercial software such as Norton Anti-virus.

3. General Software Installation

Some software programs are quite hard to install and set-up so they work properly. An example of this is a tv tuner card, we will set it up so you will recieve a clear picture and be able to record programs for a small fee.
All hardware needs drivers to make them work, and sometimes it is hard to get the correct drivers to make the hardware function properly. We will find and install the correct drivers to get you back 'up-and-running' again.

4. Hardware Repairs

If you have a perfectly fine peice of hardware that is a bit 'on the blink' or needs a bit of a tune-up, bring it into us and we will perform magic wizardry to get it back as good as new for a small price.

5. Hardware Upgrades

Our skilled team can perform quick hardware upgrades to increase the speed or usablility of your computer. For details on the services we provide, see our products page. All our technicians are skilled in this area and your data is guaranteed to be returned untouched.

6. Small Office/Home Office Consulting Service

As well as being a computer retailer, it also has a consulting service, run by the owner Bob and senior technician, Al. This service is targeted toward small offices and homes that need to be fully networked with multiple machines.
The service comprises of the following:
  • Installation ethernet cable. This includes the installation of flush boxes and hiding the cable away as much as possible. The cable used is Cat6, so the latest gigabit ethernet cards can be used to their full potential.
  • Installation of wireless (optional). Wireless can be install in the office allow laptops and PDA's to have access to local network facilities such as internet, files and printing.
  • General IT Audits. We can assess your current and future IT needs with this comprehensive service. This will help you plan for future investments and work smarter. We can also assess your current use of technology to see how it could be put to better use, or how additional technology may benfit your business.
